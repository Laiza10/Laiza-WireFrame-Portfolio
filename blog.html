ADOBO

This quintessential Filipino dish is a favorite due to its versatility and rich, comforting flavors.

SINIGANG

As a lover of sour flavors, sinigang is a clear favorite for its tangy, comforting goodness.

FRIEDCHICKEN

ried chicken is a comfort food classic that never fails to hit the spot with its crispy coating and juicy, tender meat.

LUMPIANG SHANGHAI

These crispy, golden-brown spring rolls hold a special place in my heart for their nostalgic appeal and crowd-pleasing taste.

LECHON

My Dream Country

KOREA

Known for its rich culture, delicious cuisine, advanced technology, and bustling cities like Seoul. Korea offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity, making it appealing to many travelers.

JAPAN

Famous for its stunning natural landscapes, fascinating blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge technology, delicious food, and incredibly polite and hospitable people.

PARIS

Often referred to as the "City of Love" and the "City of Lights," Paris is renowned for its romantic ambiance, iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum, world-class cuisine, fashion, art, and architecture.

LOS AANGELES, CALIFORNIA, UNITED STATES.

As the entertainment capital of the world, Hollywood draws people in with its glitz, glamour, and the allure of the film industry.

SWITZERLAND

Renowned for its breathtaking Alpine scenery, charming villages, outdoor recreational activities like skiing and hiking, and high quality of life.
